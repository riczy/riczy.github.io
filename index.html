Our Company

At Riczy, we are technology geeks with a zeal for building products.

Delivering solutions

We build mobile apps, web applications and web service APIs. We have a diverse set of experiences with years of building technical solutions. As a result, we are not tied to a single technology or language. We use the best tools to create the end product.

Design. Implement. Test. Deploy. Repeat.

We know that having great technical know-how is only one part of the equation for a successful product delivery; managing the product lifecycle is another. We use a lean, iterative development approach where a subset of project work is addressed in one to three week cycles. This allows us to be focused and flexible. Products become demonstrable early so that our customers can provide valueable feedback at each step. We help our customers achieve their product goals by establishing communication and interaction throughout the product development lifecycle.

Being talented, organized, quality-focused, and enthusiastic just comes easily for us.
